Design Proposal

Nous Deux

The Two of Us

A composition of two identical stones, set in parallel and held in measured relation. Equal in cut, proportion, and presence, each remains distinct—separated by a deliberate interval, and aligned along a diagonal axis.

La Vie Rosé

An alternate expression of Nous Deux. Two identical diamonds, held apart yet joined by a single line—a winding path of pink. A connection defined by something precious and tender: pink diamonds.

Concept

Nous Deux presents two equal stones, without hierarchy. Identical in scale, orientation, and light, they remain distinct, separated by a deliberate space that defines balance and structure.

Form

The ring studies proportion: two identical elements aligned diagonally create movement, while parallel placement preserves order, balance, and architectural clarity.

Context

Where Toi et Moi expresses a dialogue between two distinct elements, Nous Deux presents a unified pair. Two stones, equal and aligned—not in opposition, but in balance.

A Motif for a Collection

Nous Deux defines a clear, adaptable motif: parallel identical stones with controlled spacing, translated across rings, earrings, and pendants, each preserving the integrity of the original idea.

“It’s not a Toi et Moi — you and me.
It’s Nous Deux — the two of us.”
